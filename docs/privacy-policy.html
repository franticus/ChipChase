Privacy Policy

At Celestia Studios, we are deeply committed to protecting your personal data and upholding your privacy rights. This document outlines how we manage the information we collect when you interact with our website or contact our team.

1. Information We May Collect

Depending on how you use our site or services, we may collect:

  • Contact information such as your name, email address, company, and job role.
  • Device and usage data including IP address, browser type, and session duration.
  • Website interaction data like pages visited and time spent.
  • Cookies and local storage details for functionality and performance enhancements.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

We use collected data exclusively to:

  • Provide and improve our services and website features.
  • Respond to your inquiries and deliver requested content or updates.
  • Analyze user behavior to enhance usability and content quality.
  • Ensure compliance with legal obligations and maintain system security.

3. How We Safeguard Your Data

Your privacy is our priority. All sensitive information is transmitted securely, stored on protected systems, and accessible only to authorized personnel.

If a data incident ever occurs, we will promptly inform affected users and follow all applicable regulations.

4. When and With Whom We Share Data

We share personal data only when absolutely necessary, for example:

  • With service providers like hosting platforms and analytics partners under confidentiality agreements.
  • With authorities if required by law or legal proceedings.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You retain full control over your personal information. You may contact us at any time to:

  • Request access to your stored data.
  • Ask for corrections or updates.
  • Request deletion or restriction of processing.
  • Withdraw consent or object to data use.

To initiate any request, please email us at contact@celestiastudios.com.

6. Communication Preferences

If you've subscribed to updates from us, you may receive occasional emails. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the link in those messages or contacting us directly.

7. Legal Compliance

We operate in line with global privacy standards including the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act. Our practices are regularly reviewed to ensure compliance.

8. Contact Details

For any questions regarding this policy or to exercise your rights, contact us at: contact@celestiastudios.com

We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries in a timely and transparent manner.
